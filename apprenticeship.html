After my college course I pursued an apprenticeship in software development instead of going to University as I felt it was the best move for me to learn in a work environment, also I had heard from other apprentices that they had enjoyed there course and were know working in full time development roles. I did my apprenticeship with MrSite where I worked primarily on their website building software called Omnibuilder which was an Angular application where I worked on the backend APIs and services as well as some MVC applications built around the application. I enjoyed my time at MrSite but felt it was time to move on to different challenges.
